Since November 2024, I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate (PDRA) at Durham University in the Probability and Statistics group, under the supervision of Ellen Powell.

Prior to this, I completed my PhD at the Laboratoire d'Analyse et Mathématiques Appliquées (UPEC, Paris 12), under the supervision of Quentin Berger and Arnaud Le Ny.

My domain of research is Probability, focusing on statistical mechanics and random graphs.

From May to August 2025 you can find me at the Hausdorff Institute for Mathematics in Bonn as a participant of the Trimester Program "Probabilistic Methods in Quantum Field Theory".
