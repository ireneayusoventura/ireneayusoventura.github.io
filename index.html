Irene Ayuso Ventura

Since November 2024, I am a Postdoctoral Researcher Associate (PDRA) Durham University in the Probability and Statistics group, under the supervision of Ellen Powell.

Prior to this, I completed my PhD at the Laboratoire d'Analyse et Mathématiques Appliquées, under the supervision of Quentin Berger and Arnaud Le Ny.

My domain of research is Probability, and more precisely, statistical mechanics and random graphs.
